\subsection*{Focus for the First Read}

In this first read, I'm more interested in the following:\\

\textbf{Story \& Pacing}
\begin{itemize}
 \item Is the story interesting to read, is it fun, or does it feel like a chore?
 \item Is the story understandable?
 \item Where is the flow off? Where do you get stuck reading?
 \item Are there any plot-holes?
\end{itemize}

\textbf{World-Building \& Context}
\begin{itemize}
 \item Is the world interesting?
 \item Can you understand the context(without knowing too much of macroeconomics), and draw connections to our real world?
\end{itemize}

\vspace{1em}

Essentially, sentence structure and grammar are not the purpose of this step. It's about the quality of the narrative, how sound the plot is, and how engaging it is to read!
